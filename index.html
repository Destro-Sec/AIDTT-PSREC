AIDTT - 2023 is an International Conference in the field of Big data, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Cloud Computing, Digital Twins, and Edge Computing, focusing to address issues and developments in recent approaches, algorithms, mechanisms, and developments in a variety of engineering and science applications.

This conference seeks to bring together international researchers to present papers and generate discussions on the recent trends and developments of computing, communications, and science information systems.
The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and digital twin technology has revolutionized the way we design, operate, and maintain physical systems. In today's digital era, the combination of AI and digital twin allows us to create virtual replicas of real-world systems, which can be analyzed and optimized using sophisticated algorithms and machine learning techniques.
